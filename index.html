Develop

Interested in creating the next TigerApp?

TigerApps is a student-run organization that maintains and supports student-developed web applications. We cover all technological costs associated with the apps and advertise them on our homepage.
There are 3 criteria for your app to become part of TigerApps.

Relates to student life on campus

Fully functional & no known bugs

Built using modern frameworks
